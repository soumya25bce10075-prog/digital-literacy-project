# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's (5 Things)

1. Think before you post – Consider how it reflects on you
2. Use privacy settings – Control who sees your content
3. Keep profiles professional – Use a clear photo and appropriate bio
4. Connect with mentors and professionals – Build your network
5. Share meaningful content – Post about achievements and learning

## ❌ DON'Ts (5 Things)

1. Don't share personal information – Address, phone number, bank details
2. Don't engage in online arguments – Stay respectful
3. Don't post inappropriate content – Avoid offensive language or photos
4. Don't accept unknown friend requests – Be selective
5. Don't overshare – Avoid posting every detail of your personal life

## 💡 Remember
Your social media is your digital footprint. Future employers and colleges may check your profiles. Stay safe and stay professional!
